just a taste

a amateur collection of thoughts



the cure;

for you are the poison

designed to slaughter my loneliness.




beautymark;

constellations on her skin,

inviting you in,

leaving little clues,

where to go,

what to do.




yours;

i am your guilty indulgence

folded politely on a reachable shelf,

catching light from the flickering bulb

in the closet of your memories.
